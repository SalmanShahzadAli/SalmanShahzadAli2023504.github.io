üìñ About HealthMate

HealthMate is an innovative AI-powered hospital management system designed to revolutionize patient-doctor interactions. Our platform leverages advanced artificial intelligence to provide preliminary medical consultation through specialized chatbots, making healthcare more accessible and efficient for the GIKI community.

The system features domain-restricted AI chatbots trained on medical datasets, offering 24/7 availability for initial health assessments and guiding patients to the appropriate specialized care when needed.
